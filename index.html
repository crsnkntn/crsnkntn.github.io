Program Synthesis with LLM-Sourced Heuristics

An attempt to improve the asymptotic performance of Google Deepmind's TensorFlow Coder (https://arxiv.org/abs/2003.09040), a bottom-up enumerative search for TensorFlow programs.

My implementation was altogether faster and it found a few more solutions in the test suite.

Langset: Vocabulary and Phrase Flashcard Generation

In the dark days before Chat-GPT, this GPT-3-based product would generate lists of translation pairs for over 40 languages. The neat part was the ability to curate lists and export them to Anki.

Unfortunately this product is no longer active since it was hosted on Heroku before they removed the free tier.

I would rewrite it but, since GPT-4 does this out of the box, that seems wasteful.
